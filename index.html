Haddad Family Hummus Recipe

By Oz Rafer

Published November 5th, 2025

Total time: 20-30 minutes

Prep time: 5 minutes

Rating: 5/5 stars

I'm unsure of the origin of this recipe. Hummus has a pretty universal general recipe. Generally, Arabs use only 4-5 ingredients for traditional, nourishing hummus. These ingredients include chickpeas, tahini paste, lemon, garlic, salt. Traditionally, hummus would be grinded by hand. Now, most use a food processor.

While the measurements given are good starting place, hummus flavor varies based on personal taste and sensibility. I recommend adding more tahini paste than you think you need. I am really in love with that flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of chickpeas.

  • 2 tablespoons of Tahini paste.

  • 1 clove of garlic. click to remove!

  • 1/2 lemon's juice.

  • Salt to taste

  • Glass of ice water.

  • Palestinian olive oil to taste.

Thoughts?

Directions

  1. Add chickpeas to blender. Blend until the chickpeas are consistent paste.

  2. Add tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and salt.

  3. Adjust to taste. Tip: add more tahini!

  4. Once the taste is right, pour in spoonfuls of water while blending until a smooth consistency is reached.

  5. Let the blender run for 5 minutes.

  6. Allow the hummus to rest for 10 minutes.

  7. Serve in bowl with heaps of Palestinian olive oil, your preferred spices, and warm pita.
